How to outline a novel without overcomplicating it

1. start with the ending
Know where you're taking readers

2. Give your protagonist a clear goal.
No goal = no story.

3. Write down 5 to 10 major events.
Only the moments that change everything

4. Make every event cause the next one.
Coincidences weaken plots.

5. Ask one question.
What makes things worse from here?

A good outline doesn't tell you everything.

It gives you a direction worth following
